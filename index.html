Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

The Hunt App (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a real-world AR adventure game. We value your privacy and take it seriously.

Information We Collect

  • Email and username you provide for registration and login.
  • Location data while using the Hunter Lens (only when enabled by you).
  • Device identifiers and analytics for app performance and security.
  • Subscription and purchase data (through App Store / Play Store billing).

How We Use Information

  • To operate core features such as gameplay, AR location, and prizes.
  • To send push notifications and gameplay updates you opt into.
  • To maintain account security and improve app experience.

Sharing of Data

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing occurs with service providers like cloud hosting (AWS), push notifications (Firebase), and payment processors.

Your Choices

You may revoke permissions (camera, location, notifications) in device settings at any time. For account deletion or questions, contact us below.
